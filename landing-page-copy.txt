PLAYBOOK (How It Works) — #approach
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Section label: How It Works

Headline:
Start with one company.
Then scale the playbook.

Intro: We identify the portfolio company with the clearest operational pain. Deploy a small team of engineers for 6-8 weeks. Build, launch, and measure. Once you see the results, we roll the same system across the rest of your portfolio faster and cheaper.

Step /01 — Identify
Work with your operating team to select the portfolio company with the biggest operational drag: manual data entry, paper-based workflows, fragmented legacy systems. We narrow the scope to one specific problem with a measurable outcome.

Step /02 — Embed & Build
A small team of engineers operates from inside the portfolio company. We map the workflow, design the AI system, and build production infrastructure. No consultants, no decks. Just working code that runs.

Step /03 — Deploy & Measure
Go live in 6-8 weeks. Measure the output: headcount freed up, cost per transaction cut in half, days of manual work eliminated. Show the impact in terms that matter to your LPs.

Step /04 — Expand Across the Portfolio
Once you have proof of concept, we deploy the same AI system to your next portfolio company. Timeline drops to 4-6 weeks. Each subsequent deployment is cheaper. What took 8 weeks for company one takes 3 weeks for company ten.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Section label: What We Build

Headline:
AI that hits your P&L

Intro: Every system we deploy targets a specific cost center or operational bottleneck. We measure success in dollars saved and headcount freed. Not in activity or consulting hours.

Workflow Automation
Replace data entry, spreadsheet management, and repetitive back-office work with AI systems that run 24/7. The tasks your team spends 40 hours a week on get executed in seconds.

Customer Service & Sales
AI voice and chat agents that handle inbound inquiries, route calls to the right person, pull from your knowledge base, and execute transaction workflows. Cuts support headcount by 30-50% without dropping service quality.

Document Processing
Invoices, contracts, tax forms, insurance claims. Automatically extracted, classified, and routed. Eliminates weeks of manual data entry and paper shuffling in finance, insurance, and services companies.

Reporting & Analytics
Monthly financial reporting and operational dashboards built automatically from your existing systems. Operating partners see real-time visibility instead of waiting for month-end close.

Sales & Marketing Automation
AI-powered prospecting, lead scoring, CRM workflow automation, and outbound sequences. Replace outdated sales processes with systems that scale.

Legacy System Integration
Connect fragmented ERP, CRM, and custom software through data pipelines. Your systems can finally talk to each other without a full rip-and-replace.
